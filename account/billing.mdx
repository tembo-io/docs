## Subscription Details

Monthly subscriptions are paid in advance through Stripe.

## Additional Solve Packs

Extra solve packs are available for purchase:
- $10 for 10 solves
- $20 for 25 solves
- $75 for 100 solves
- $200 for 300 solves
