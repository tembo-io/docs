---

Tembo is the Zapier for AI engineering tasks.

Create [workflows](/docs/workflows) that automatically handle code maintenance, bug fixes, system optimizations and more. When an engineering issue occurs, Tembo solves it with AI.

## How it works

Tembo is powered by AI to:

- Integrate with your stack
- Detect issues and open PRs to fix them
- Optimize and maintain your code, database and infrastructure continuously
