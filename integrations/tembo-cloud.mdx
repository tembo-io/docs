## No work required

You don't have to do anything to connect dba to your Tembo Cloud Postgres instances, because dba shares Tembo's authentication service with Tembo Cloud.

After signing in with your Tembo Cloud account, you'll simply need to connect Github repos to begin recieving PRs.
